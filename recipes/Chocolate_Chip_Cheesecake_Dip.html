Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Dip

This cheesecake dip is heavenly! Imagine a chocolate chip cheesecake batter that is safe to eat.
This dip will quickly become a family favorite. Serve with graham crackers.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips

The steps needed for making the Chocolate Chip Cheesecake Dip

  1. Beat together cream cheese and butter in a medium bowl with an electric mixer until smooth,
  2. Mix in confectioners' sugar,
  3. and vanilla until well combined,
  4. Stir in chocolate chips until evenly incorporated,
  5. Keep dip refrigerated until ready to serve
